Shipping & Returns

Shipping Policy

We ship worldwide. Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days.

Return Policy

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

How to Return

To initiate a return, please contact our support team at support@hijabclothing.com with your order number.

Refunds

Once we receive your return, we will inspect it and notify you that we have received your returned item. We will immediately notify you on the status of your refund after inspecting the item.
